Welcome

The project consists of verifying the authenticity of diplomas issued by a training institution. The objective is to ensure that the diplomas issued by the institution are well recognized and have a value on the labor market. To this end, the To do this, the project will put in place control measures to ensure that the diplomas are indeed issued by the institution and that their content is consistent with the requirements of the training program. Indeed, this control measure is based on Blockchain technology. All diplomas will be stored on a blockchain, in our case Ethereum.

This project can be used in two different ways:


Add a degree


Every year, students will graduate. The institution will add their diplomas in the blockchain to certify it.

You will have to go to the "Sign in" option at the top right of the page to authenticate yourself and add your degree(s).

Check if a degree is recognized by the establishment

In the case where a person wants to prove that his diploma is well recognized by his institution, he will check that it is well on the blockchain of his institution.

The student or company wishing to verify a diploma should go to the "Verification" page at the top right of the page

Note that it is not necessary to be authenticated to verify this.
